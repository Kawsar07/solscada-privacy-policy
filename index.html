Our App collects and processes operational data including:

  • Real-Time Monitoring Data: Live AC/DC power readings, voltage, current, frequency measurements
  • Energy Generation Data: Energy production (kWh), daily/monthly/annual generation totals
  • Performance Metrics: Performance Ratio (PR) values, capacity factor, system efficiency

7.2 Why We Use Role-Based Access Control

We implement different user roles for critical security, privacy, and operational reasons:

  • Authentication & Authorization: Ensures only authentic, authorized personnel access solar plant data by requiring admin verification and approval
  • Identity Verification: Admins verify the identity and legitimacy of users before granting access, preventing unauthorized individuals from viewing sensitive data
  • Data Security: Users access only information relevant to their responsibilities, reducing unauthorized data exposure
  • Privacy Protection: Prevents unnecessary access to sensitive company and plant data through organizational hierarchy
  • Principle of Least Privilege: Users receive minimum necessary access for their role, meeting compliance requirements (GDPR, LGPD, PIPEDA)
  • Accountability: Creates clear audit trails tracking who accesses specific data and features
  • Multi-Company Support: Serves multiple companies while keeping their data completely separate and secure
  • Scalability: Organizations can grow and add users without compromising security or control
  • Regulatory Compliance: Helps meet data protection requirements globally

7.3 User Approval Process - Security & Verification

How User Accounts Are Created and Approved:

SolScada requires administrator approval to ensure only authentic, authorized personnel gain access to solar plant monitoring data. This is a security measure, not a payment gate.

  • Step 1 - User Registration: New user registers or receives invitation
  • Step 2 - Pending Status: Account enters pending state awaiting admin approval
  • Step 3 - Verification: Admin/Super Admin verifies user's identity, legitimacy, employment status, and authorization level
  • Step 4 - Decision:
    • Approved: Verified authentic user receives account with appropriate role and permissions
    • Rejected: Unverified or unauthorized user is denied access for security protection
  • Step 5 - Access Granted: Approved users can access only data they're authorized to view based on assigned role

Approval Wait Time: New users may wait for administrator approval. This wait time varies based on admin availability but ensures only verified, authentic personnel access sensitive solar plant data.

Why This Matters: This process prevents unauthorized access, protects proprietary data, ensures security compliance, and verifies users are legitimate employees, contractors, or stakeholders.
