Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

SCUBE Technologies Ltd. We operates the SolScada mobile application the App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using SolScada, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Free App & No Payments

SolScada is completely FREE.
• No subscription fees
• No in-app purchases
• No advertisements
• No payment or billing information collected

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account or use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional), password, and physical address (optional)
  • Company Information: Company name and company logo/image
  • Site Information: Site name, site IP address, and site images
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you choose to provide in your profile

1.2 Solar Plant Data

Our App collects and processes solar energy monitoring data including:

  • Live AC/DC power readings
  • Energy generation data (kWh)
  • Performance Ratio (PR) values
  • Module temperature readings
  • Irradiation measurements (W/m²)
  • Inverter status and data
  • Revenue calculations (BDT)
  • Historical trends and analytics
  • Plant layout and Single Line Diagram (SLD) information
  • Total installed capacity (kWp)

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • IP addresses of configured solar monitoring systems
  • App usage data and interaction logs
  • Crash reports and performance data

1.4 Location Data

We may collect location information if you provide your address during registration or grant location permissions for site-related features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve the SolScada monitoring services
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account
  • Solar Monitoring: To display real-time and historical solar plant performance data
  • Analytics: To generate energy production reports, revenue calculations, and performance analytics
  • Communication: To send important notifications about your solar systems, including alerts and performance reports
  • Technical Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain data integrity
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you have given us explicit permission
  • Company Administrators: If you are part of a company account, administrators may access relevant data
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who assist in operating our services (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure password storage with hashing algorithms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with data backup

Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Solar monitoring data is retained according to the following schedule:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion is requested
  • Solar Performance Data: Historical data retained for analytics and reporting purposes
  • Technical Logs: Retained for up to 12 months for troubleshooting and security

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by accessing the menu from your profile icon (first letter of your name) at the top of the app screen, then selecting "Delete Account" from the dropdown options.

6.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data

  • Object to Processing: Object to certain types of data processing

6.2 For European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
  • Right to data portability

6.3 For California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access their personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Equal service and price, even after exercising privacy rights

6.4 Account Management

To manage your account settings:

  • Tap on your profile icon (displaying the first letter of your name) at the top of the screen
  • A dropdown menu will appear with the following options:
    • Personal Info: View your account information
    • Update Profile: Modify your personal information
    • Change Password: Update your account password
    • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account and associated data
    • Logout: Sign out of your account
  • You can also manage company and site information through their respective sections
